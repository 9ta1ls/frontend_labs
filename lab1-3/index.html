Skyler Knutzen

Skyler Knutzen is a New York based Director, DP, and Editor interested in the ways everyday life is wrapped up in memory, mortality, and familial connections.

His work has been screened at SXSW 2023, Netflix (The New Americans), Sundance Film Festival 2022, Paramount + (Last Flight Home), Cinequest Film Festival 2021, WCFF 2021, Queens World Film Festival 2021 (Daughter Sun), Nowness, Vimeo Staff Pick (Elvis Isn't Dead) and others.

Partial client list: Maybelline, Interscope Records, Lincoln Center, Great Big Story, Nars Cosmetics, The Guardian, and more.
